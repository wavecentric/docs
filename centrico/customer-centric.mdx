The **Customer Centric** module is the heart of CENTRICO's CRM capabilities, focusing on high-quality master data and financial transparency.

## Components

### Customers & Contacts
Centralized management of all business entities (Subjects). This goes beyond a simple list by integrating deep financial data.
- **CreditSafe Integration**: Real-time financial health monitoring, international credit scores, and risk assessments.
- **Network Visualization**: Map relationships between companies, parent groups, and individual users.

![Customer Management](/images/customer-management.png)

### Contracts
Management of commercial agreements and service level specifications.
- **Integrated Search**: Quickly locate contracts by customer, validity, or service type.
- **AI-Driven Analysis**: Summarize and extract key terms from complex contractual documents.

### Analytics & Reports
Dedicated commercial reporting to track customer performance and engagement.
- **Volume Tracking**: Monitor throughput per customer.
- **Engagement Metrics**: Track inquiry conversion rates.

## Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Lead Acquisition">
    New potential customers are entered into the system or captured via the public API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Financial Validation">
    The system automatically links the subject to its CreditSafe profile to verify solvency and recommended credit limits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contract Implementation">
    Commercial terms are finalized and stored, enabling the quotation and execution modules to apply the correct rates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
