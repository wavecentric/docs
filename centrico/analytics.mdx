CENTRICO provides high-level executive dashboards to monitor the health of the commercial pipeline and the overall performance of the logistics network.

## KPI Dashboard

The **KPI Dashboard** provides real-time metrics on operational and commercial health. It aggregates data from across the platform to provide actionable insights.

- **Booking Trends**: Monitor the volume of bookings over time (Daily, Weekly, Monthly).
- **Service Utilization**: Track how well your rail, trucking, and maritime services are being filled.
- **Customer Analysis**: Identify your top-performing clients and growth opportunities.

![Analytics Dashboard](/images/analytics-reports.png)

## Sales Funnel

The **Sales Funnel** is a specialized visualization tool that tracks the conversion of leads into active logistics operations.

### Funnel Stages
1. **Leads**: Prospects identified.
2. **Inquiries**: Specific service requests received and validated.
3. **Quotations**: Commercial offers sent to customers.
4. **Converted (Won)**: Offers that resulted in active bookings.
5. **Loss Analysis**: Understand why quotes were rejected (e.g., Price, Lead Time, Equipment Availability).

![Sales Funnel](/images/sales-funnel.png)

## Data Driven Decisions
By using these analytics tools, managers can:
- **Identify Bottlenecks**: See where inquiries are stalling in the pipeline.
- **Optimize Pricing**: Adjust quotes based on conversion data and market trends.
- **Service Planning**: Use historical booking data to plan future service frequency in the **Orchestrator**.
