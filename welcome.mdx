## Logging In

AI Loop uses a secure, passwordless authentication method to ensure a frictionless and safe user experience.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Login Page">
    Go to <a href="https://ai-loop.alpeadria.com" target="_blank">ai-loop.alpeadria.com</a> and click **Get Started**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Email">
    Enter your work email address. A secure login link will be sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access via Magic Link">
    Open the email from AI Loop and click the provided link to log in instantly—no password required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info icon="key">
  **Security Notice:** AI Loop uses time-sensitive magic links and signed JWT (JSON Web Tokens) to ensure a secure, passwordless login experience.
</Info>

<Info icon="shield-check">
  **Administrator Approval Required:** New users must be approved by an administrator before accessing the platform.
</Info>
