## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Application">
    Navigate to [ai-loop.alpeadria.com](https://ai-loop.alpeadria.com) and select **Get Started**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identity Verification">
    Input your corporate email address to receive a secure, encrypted verification link delivered to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Click the **"Verify & Login Now"** button in the email to access the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Provide your professional details (Name, Company, Job Title, and Location) to finish setting up your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
