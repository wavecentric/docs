The **Contatti** registry manages the communication points for all physical and legal entities in the system.

## Key Features
- **Person Registry**: Name, surname, and professional roles.
- **Communication Channels**: Direct links to email and mobile numbers for automated orchestration.
- **Subject Association**: Every contact is linked to a primary **Subject** (Customer, Carrier, etc.).

## Usage
- **Mail Triggers**: Contacts are pulled automatically when sending status updates via `MAGEMO_MAIL_MODELS`.
- **Task Assignment**: Used to identify the correct recipient for operational alerts.
