The **Documenti** registry allows administrators to define the categories of documents required for various transport operations.

## Key Features
- **Mandatory Flags**: Define which document types are required for specific services or commodities.
- **Categorization**: Group documents by role (e.g., Customs, Safety, Contractual).
- **Template Linking**: Link document types to digital templates for automated generation.

## Common Types
- **CIM**: International Rail Consignment Note.
- **CMR**: Road Consignment Note.
- **RID**: Dangerous Goods Declaration.
- **BL**: Bill of Lading for maritime legs.
