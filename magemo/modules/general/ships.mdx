The **Navi** registry maintains technical specifications for maritime assets involved in multimodal journeys.

## Key Components
- **Vessel Registry**: Detailed data for individual ships (Name, IMO, Flag).
- **Vessel Types**: Categorization of ships (e.g., Feeder, Mother Vessel, Ro-Ro).
- **Carrier Links**: Association of vessels with specific maritime shipping lines.

## Technical Specs
- **Capacity**: Container capacity and draft limits.
- **Provider Hub**: Integration with PCS for automated vessel arrival/departure schedules.
