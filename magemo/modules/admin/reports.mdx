The **Report operativi** section provides detailed exports and analysis for specific logistics domains.

## 1. Train Volumetric Reports
This report visualizes the volumes transported per train over a specific period. It is essential for analyzing load factors and service utilization.

## 2. Customer Performance Reports
Aggregate data based on specific client mandates:
- **Tonnage by Client**: Total volume transported within a date range.
- **Service Frequency**: How often a client utilizes specific nodes or traces.

## 3. Asset & Stoppage Reports
- **Report Soste**: Analyzing idle time for wagons and units at various terminals.
- **Report Autotrasportatori**: Evaluating the performance and mandate fulfillment of road partners.

## 4. Statistics Panel
A consolidated view of system-wide performance metrics, allowing for dynamic filtering and data extraction to CSV/Excel.
