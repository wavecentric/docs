The **Fleet V1** section provides a historical and status-based view of the rail fleet.

## 1. Flotta Carri
A detailed grid view of the physical wagon fleet, including their current location and technical metadata.
- **Search & Filter**: Find wagons by number, type, or terminal.

## 2. Stato Carri
Visual monitoring of the asset state across the network.
- **Operational Availability**: Tracking which wagons are active vs. transitioning between nodes.
- **In-Transit Analysis**: Status of wagons currently assigned to active trips.
