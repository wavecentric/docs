The **Fleet V2** section focuses on high-performance data discovery and operational foresight.

## 1. Search and View
A powerful, multi-parameter search engine for the entire asset pool.
- **Cross-Registry Search**: Search simultaneously across Wagons, UTIs, and pending missions.
- **Deep Visibility**: Drill down from a high-level fleet summary into individual asset history.

## 2. Integrated Fleet Intelligence
Combines IoT data (Track & Trace) with the static registry to provide a live view of asset health and geographical density.
