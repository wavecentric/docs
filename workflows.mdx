## Workflows

A **workflow** is an automated process designed to handle repetitive tasks for you. Each workflow is represented by a card on your dashboard, giving you a central hub to manage all your automations.

### Getting Started from Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is the central hub for all your automations. From here, you can quickly find what you need or start a new task.

-   **Search for Workflows:** Use the search bar at the top of the page to instantly filter and find the exact workflow you're looking for.
-   **Use the Query Assistant:** For direct help , use the **Query Assistant**. This powerful tool allows you to ansuer questions or state goals in natural language to quickly generate results.

### Workflow Card

The workflow card gives you direct access to run, view, or edit any workflow.
