The AI Loop platform provides integrated support channels, secure session controls, and public contact avenues to ensure a seamless experience for both authenticated users and external partners.

## In-App Help & Support
Accessible directly from the primary navigation sidebar, the **Help & Support** module provides immediate access to platform documentation and technical assistance.
- **Direct Documentation**: Jump to the comprehensive AI Loop knowledge base at [docs.wavecentric.ai](https://docs.wavecentric.ai) from any page.
- **Technical Assistance**: Reach out to the WaveCentric support team for workflow troubleshooting or account issues.

## Session Management
Securely manage your platform access through the user profile menu located at the bottom of the navigation sidebar.

### User Menu Options
- **Profile Overview**: View your active email and profile avatar.
- **Settings**: Quick link to your [Account & Membership Settings](/functionalities/membership-account-management).
- **Theme Selection**: Toggle between Light, Dark, and high-contrast visual modes.
- **Log Out**: Terminate your active session securely. This action clears your local authentication state and redirects you to the secure login page.

## Public Contact (Let's Talk)
For general inquiries, technical support, or business partnerships, external stakeholders can use the public **Contact Us** portal at [ai-loop.alpeadria.com/contact](https://ai-loop.alpeadria.com/contact).

### Inquiry Orchestration
- **Comprehensive Form**: Submit your Full Name, Email, and message to trigger a formal service request.
- **Guided Submission**: The interface ensures all required fields are validated before submission.
- **Legal Compliance**: Integrated acceptance of **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy** to ensure data governance.
