## What It Does

The AI Assistant acts as an intelligent companion within the application, capable of:

- **Answering Questions**: Query internal databases (tasks, team data, configurations) and get instant answers
- **Flow Navigation**: Suggests relevant flows based on your question and provides descriptions
- **Personalized Responses**: Tailors answers based on your name, role, and assigned tasks
- **Contextual Understanding**: Analyzes your query and provides accurate, focused responses

## How to Use

Simply type your question in natural language, such as:

- "What tasks are assigned to me?"
- "Show me flows related to fleet management"
- "Who is on the admin team?"
- "What can I do with the communication system?"

The assistant will analyze your query, search relevant data sources, and provide a clear, formatted response with the information you need.

## Key Features

- **Smart Data Retrieval**: Automatically searches across multiple internal databases
- **Flow Recommendations**: Suggests applicable flows when they match your query topic
- **User Context**: Recognizes who you are and customizes responses accordingly
- **Formatted Output**: Presents answers with proper structure, bullet points, and spacing for easy readability
