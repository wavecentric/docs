**CreditSafe** provides instant access to detailed financial reports and credit risk assessments for companies across multiple countries. Evaluate the financial stability and business reliability of potential partners, suppliers, or customers.

## What It Does

CreditSafe integration enables you to:

- **Search Companies**: Find companies by name and country from an extensive global database
- **Financial Reports**: Generate comprehensive credit reports with financial data, risk scores, and business insights
- **Multi-Country Coverage**: Access data across 15+ countries including EU, US, Canada, and Japan
- **Risk Assessment**: Evaluate financial stability and creditworthiness before doing business

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### 1. Enter Company Details
    Input the company name and select the country from supported regions:
    - **European**: Austria, Italy, France, Belgium, Netherlands, Germany, Luxembourg, Denmark, Sweden, Norway, UK, Ireland
    - **Americas**: United States, Canada
    - **Asia**: Japan
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### 2. Select Company
    The system searches CreditSafe database and presents matching companies with addresses and locations. Choose the correct company from the list.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### 3. Generate Report
    Click "Generate Report" to retrieve comprehensive financial information including:
    - Company registration details
    - Financial statements and ratios
    - Credit rating and risk score
    - Business history and operations
    - Payment behavior and trends
  </Step>
</Steps>

- **Due Diligence**: Assess financial health before entering business relationships

- **Real-Time Data**: Get up-to-date financial information from CreditSafe's live database
