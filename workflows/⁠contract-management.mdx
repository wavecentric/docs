The Contract Management flow is designed to simplify the process of generating and dispatching official contracts to your clients. This tool guides you through creating a draft, reviewing the final document, and sending it out.

The workflow is organized into three primary actions:
1.  **Create Contract**
2.  **View Contract**
3.  **Send Contract**

---

## 1. Create Contract

This initial step involves creating a draft of the contract by providing essential client and service information.

<Callout>
  **Description:** Creates a draft Contract by collecting client details and drafting the contract.
</Callout>

### Input Fields

To begin, fill out the required information in the form below. Fields marked with a red asterisk (*) are mandatory.

| Field Name           | Description                                    | Required |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | :------: |
| **Select a Cliente** | Choose the client for whom the contract is being prepared. |   Yes    |
| **Select a servizio**| Select the service that the contract will cover. |   Yes    |

After filling in the details, click the **Run** the user will receive button for show view contract.

---

## 2. View Contract

Once a draft has been created, Contract_id allows you to preview the complete contract. It's a critical step to ensure all details are correct and formatted as expected before sending and they will receive button for send contract
<Callout type="info">
  **Purpose:** To show the final draft of the Contract.
</Callout>

---

## 3. Send Contract

This is the final action in the process. After a thorough review, you can use this function to formally send the contract to the client.

<Callout type="info">
  **Purpose:** To send the Contract.
</Callout>
